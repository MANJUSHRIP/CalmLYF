Gift a CalmLYF membership

Thank you for choosing CalmLYF for you and your loved one's therapy needs.

What happens next?

  • Your recipient will get your invitation via email and text (if applicable).
  • They will fill out the matching questionnaire and get matched with a therapist.
  • Once matched, they can start their therapy journey with a licensed therapist. You will not get charged until they are matched.

What if my gift recipient does not like their therapist?

Your recipient can switch therapists at any time. CalmLYF has over 32,000 therapists worldwide with different qualifications and areas of expertise.

How much will it cost?

With CalmLYF your loved one can have professional therapy for only $80 USD per week, charged every 4 weeks. CalmLYF membership includes a weekly live session (video, phone or chat), and the ability to message your therapist anytime, from any internet-connected device, wherever you are. The subscription is billed and renewed every 4 weeks, starting when your recipient is matched to a therapist.

Will I be charged now?

No. You will be charged when the gift recipient signs up in CalmLYF and is matched to a therapist.

When does the gift membership end?

You can stop covering the cost of therapy at any time. If you stop, your recipient can continue out of pocket without any interruption. We will also stop membership renewal charges if your recipient decides they want to cancel their membership.
